Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how Travelia collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when using this website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • 1.1. Name and contact information (e.g., email address, phone number) for inquiries and bookings.
  • 1.2. Demographic information, such as preferences and interests, to improve our services.
  • 1.3. Payment details for completing transactions.

2. Use of Collected Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • 2.1. To process and manage bookings, reservations, and payments.
  • 2.2. To provide personalized travel recommendations and offers.
  • 2.3. To improve our services and website based on customer feedback.
  • 2.4. To send promotional emails or newsletters, if opted in by the user.

3. Security Measures

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your personal information.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. Once you leave our site, we are not responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide on those sites.
